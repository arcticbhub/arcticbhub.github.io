About

arcticbhub is my pet project, dedicated to the crypto community.

You can post your job offer here or ask for one. As long as you accept some form of crypto as payment.

All posts are moderated and will be listed (if approved) in 2 - 24 hours under normal circumstances.

Note: posts related to illegal activities and known scams will not make it on the website. If you see anything fishy please report it via email or pull it from the website's repository.
